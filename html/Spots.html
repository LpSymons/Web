Some more local trails to check out



Poldice Valley

Hand built trails in a old mining quarry, Routes suitable for all abilities. As this site is built by fellow riders it is free to visit and ride. As this site is not managed there are no nearby facilities.

View Video

Woody's Bike Park

Woody's is an bike park with lots of professionally built trails of various levels of riders. You will have to pay entry fee or a season ticket and they also provide a uplift for a additional cost.

View Video

Oldhill Bike Park

Oldhill bike park is a small bike park that is great for beginners trying to build comfidence with progressive jumps and features. A small entry fee is required to ride this park.

View Video

The Track

The Track is a large park purpose built for jumps and high features. They include large jumps, wall rides and a foam pit. You will have to pay a entry fee and bike rental is also available on site.
